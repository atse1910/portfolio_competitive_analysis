Website # 6

Chipotle Mexican Grill
 Link: Click Here

Item Description
Type of Website Featured Graphic
Brand Chipotle is well-known for its best-tasting burritos available in the market today and what better way to show case it than a burrito on the internet. Chipotle's web site leaves users hungry and end up filled in the stomach. Chipotle's website shines it customers with the following attributes when visiting Chipotle's website:
  • Contemporary
  • Unique
  • Informative
  • Creative
Functionality Chipotle does a great job in expositioning its product to consumers. On the initial visit, a Chipotle burrito drops from the sky. Upon arrival on the ground, the burrito rocks back and forth with a sudden unwrapping to reveal the skin of this gourmet burrito. It tempts visitors to purchase its product from a simple message informing where their supply derives from to complex issues of humane treatment of animals.  Chipotle's website is unique in its own way because the website stands for the company's mission and code of ethics.
Interactivity Chipotle's interactivity with users include flash animations, user-website involvement, expand-collapse pages and the ability to allow users to be in control. Overall, Chipotle's website is highly interactive with users. It is no wonder Chipotle remains one of the best American Mexican restaurants today.
Navigation Chipotle's navigation element is a horizontal-categorized drop down navigation bar. Navigation is easily achieved as the navigation puts the user in control. Users are able to move back and forth between web pages without much delay.
